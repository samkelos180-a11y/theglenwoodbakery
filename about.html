About Us

Glenwood Bakery mainly out of passion, but also because I love the idea of bringing families together with something simple to enjoy, like biscuits they can share and snack on.This recipe actually comes from my great-grandmother, and it’s been passed down through generations until it got to me. So it’s very personal and homemade. Right now, I run the business on my own from home, and I mainly focus on freshly baked biscuits with delivery around Durban. My goal is to grow the business, reach more customers, and eventually expand into supplying shops, while still keeping that homemade feel I’d like the brand to feel warm, friendly, and welcoming something that feels like home.
